# Gameplay contract

The player loop is observation, memory, manual operation, evaluation, and progression.

- Reality shows customers and ingredients through near-sighted blur. It allows movement, pickup, and crafting.
- Glasses show a separate clear world. The player can still move, but cannot interact with customers, ingredients, or crafting stations. Hide customers and raw ingredients; retain the bar and tools with different materials and information labels.
- Keep one authoritative physical and recipe state across both worlds.
- Make measuring, pouring, grinding, extraction, mixing, ice handling, and decoration meaningful operations where applicable.
- Never let the glasses complete work, become a permanent HUD, or replace player skill.
- Prioritize experience, feedback, stability, then physical realism.
- Provide discard-and-remake recovery for crafting mistakes.
- The current slice contains one prototype iced Americano tutorial. Do not treat placeholder amounts as an approved recipe.
